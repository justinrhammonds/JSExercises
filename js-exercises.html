Factorial Fun!

A "factorial" is simply a product, which is indicated by an exclamation mark. For example, "four factorial" is written as "4!" and means 1×2×3×4 = 24. In general, n! ("n factorial") means the product of all whole numbers from 1 to n (n! = 1×2×3×...×n).
